    What is the Kmerthon?

    Kmerthon is a programming contest open to anyone. The goal is to perform k-mer counting on a set of DNA sequences.

    K-mer counting consists of enumerating all the distinct DNA strings of length k, and reporting the number of times they occur in the dataset. As this is a simple computational problem, you do not need any knowledge in bioinformatics or biology to enter this contest.

    Why a Kmerthon?

    K-mer counting is an important problem in bioinformatics. It is the first step of hundreds of tools (e.g. DNA assemblers). [...]
